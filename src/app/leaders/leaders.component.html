Rama Krishna Raju

Rama Krishna Raju is the co-founder and CEO of Pennant Technologies. Raju's passion for technology and visionary zeal led him on the entrepreneurial journey of building world-class products for the banking and financial services industry. Since its inception in 2005, Pennant Technologies has seen consistent and industry-leading growth. Over the years, the company has won numerous industry recognitions and awards.

With over 20+ years' experience purely in the financial technology space, with a major part in leading and managing transformational programs for leading banks and financial institutions in middle-east Asia, Raju brings deep domain knowledge, extensive experience, and strong leadership in retail, corporate and treasury functions of the banking industry. He has spoken at various forums and shared his deep understanding and insights on various technology and financial services topics. Raju is a member of the FICCI National Committee on Fintech.

Raju holds a degree in Computer Science and is based out of Hyderabad, India.

Pradeep Varma

Pradeep Varma is the co-founder of Pennant Technologies. Entrepreneurial instinct and passion for building technology products and solutions to address the problems and opportunities in the banking space led him to the idea of starting Pennant. Pradeep is responsible for product vision and development, and over the years has been instrumental in launching several new products that helped banks and financial institutions innovate, drive operational efficiencies and deliver superior customer experiences. Some of the products have received global recognitions including from analysts and industry bodies.

With over 20+ years' experience, Pradeep has a significant understanding and experience of the financial services industry. He has worked in a leadership position for some of the leading banks and financial institutions in middle-east Asia. Among significant accomplishments, he successfully led the transformation of the application landscape of a large bank in UAE while transitioning from a conventional bank to an Islamic Bank.

Pradeep holds a degree in Civil Engineering and is based out of Hyderabad, India.
